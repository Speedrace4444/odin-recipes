Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for Ziti that I learned from the Internet. It's very delicious and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 package of ground beef
  • 1 package of taco seasoning
  • Flat hard taco shells
  • Cheese
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Sour cream
  • Cooking spray
  • Bacon bits

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan. Add in the taco seasoning according to the instructions.
  2. Set the ground beef aside. Put down a tortilla. Put some ground beef on it, then put a hard taco shell on that.
  3. On top of the hard taco shell, put sour cream and tomatoes. Then cover it all in cheese. Put the last tortilla on top of that.
  4. Put cooking spray in a skillet and then put the tortillas in the skillet. Cook until browned.
  5. Enjoy!
