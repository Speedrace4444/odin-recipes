Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for Mac and Cheese that I learned from the Internet. It's very delicious and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of milk
  • 1 1/2 cups of cheese
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • Hot sauce
  • Salt, pepper, mustard powder
  • Bacon bits

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until cooked. Set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan. Slowly mix in the flour.
  3. Slowly mix in the heavy cream and whole milk. Bring to a boil, then bring a simmer.
  4. Mix in the cheese until a sauce is formed. Then put in all of the spices and hot sauce to taste.
  5. Slowly pour the pasta into the sauce and mix well. Add the bacon bits.
  6. Enjoy!
