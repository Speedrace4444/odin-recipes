Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for Mac and Cheese that I learned from the Internet. It's very delicious and simple to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pasta
  • 1 package of ground turkey
  • 1/2 cup of cheese
  • 1 can of marinara sauce
  • Salt, pepper, cumin, garlic powder

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until cooked. Set aside.
  2. Separately, cook the ground turkey and season until done.
  3. Combine the meat and pasta in a pot, add the marinara sauce, and season to taste with whatever seasonings you like.
  4. Add the cheese and stir.
  5. Enjoy!
